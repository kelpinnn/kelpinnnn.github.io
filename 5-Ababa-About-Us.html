Ababa Steak is an international standard restaurant initiated by 3 Binusian 24 namely; Gabriel, Kelvin, and Sebastian. A restaurant that specializes in providing high quality steaks and certainly will not disappoint our loyal customers. In addition, Ababa Steak has opened 3 branches and we have also opened new jobs for professionals to show their skills in serving the steak with an international taste, and of course using original raw materials from Indonesia. With that Ababa Steak also opens up business opportunities with local farmers to compete in providing high quality meat. Ababa Steak wants to introduce to the world that the taste and quality of Indonesian cuisine is not inferior to that of other countries.

Information

Established in 2020 Ababa Steak works with farmers throughout the archipelago to provide selected and quality beef to be processed into steak menus with global flavors. Further information can be via our social media or you can directly visit our address listed on this website.
